Top 7 Tips for Writing Great Web Content

Want to write web content that sings? Interested in making your readers come back for more? Read on for quick ways that you can write winning website text today.

  1. Know your audience. Having a good sense of your audience ensures you're picking the right vocabulary, colors, information, hyperlinks, photos, etc. that will draw attention to your content.

  2. Keep it concise, simple, and straightforward. This will keep your audience engaged, makes your text skimmable, and eliminate confusion.

  3. Use the inverted pyramid. Provide the basic info readers need upfront in case they don't stick around long. You can get into the details later.

  4. Chunk your content. Briefer paragraphs and even single sentences are easy to read and scan. Breaking up content into subsections, lists, etc. also helps create hierarchy on your site.

  5. Talk to your reader. Don't be afraid to use pronouns and talk directly to the reader. This keeps it casual and personal and is more engaging.

  6. Use active voice. It's more direct, it improves the pacing of your writing, and it's engaging too!

  7. Show, don't tell. If you tell someone something without showing, it affects your credibility. Explain why. Give readers the details they need (and want). And include relevant hyperlinks, too.
